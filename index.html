Privacy Policy

At Heaven Trinity, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user information in our mobile applications, ensuring transparency and trust.

Information We Collect

We prioritize your privacy by design. Our applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data generated by the app, such as user preferences or app-generated content, is stored locally on your device and remains under your control. No data is sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

How We Use Data

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not process or use any information about you. The app leverages locally stored data solely to deliver its core functionality, ensuring a seamless and private user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user information with third parties, as no personal data is collected. Your privacy is fully protected, and no external entities have access to any app-related data.

Security Measures

While we do not collect personal data, we implement robust security practices to ensure the integrity of our applications. Any locally stored data is protected by your device’s built-in security features, such as encryption and access controls.

Children’s Privacy

Our applications are designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of collecting data from minors, ensuring compliance with applicable regulations like COPPA.

Changes to This Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
